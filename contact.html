// Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a project take?

Timelines vary by scope. A custom website typically takes 3–6 weeks. Web applications can take 8–16 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline after scoping.

Do you work with clients outside South Africa?

Yes — we work with clients globally. Our team operates remotely and we communicate clearly across time zones.

What's your payment structure?

We typically require a 50% deposit to start, with the balance on project delivery. Payment plans are available for larger projects.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Absolutely. All projects include a 30-day post-launch support window. We also offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing maintenance and growth.
